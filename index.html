Sasinda Chandula

Lecturer & Researcher · Sri Lanka · +94 71 239 1067 ·

I am currently a Lecturer (Probationary) in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the Faculty of Engineering , University of Jaffna , Sri Lanka. I completed my undergraduate study at University of Jaffna, with a First Class in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. I worked as an Academic Instructor and later as an Assistant Lecturer in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering. I did my internship as an Associate Electronic Engineer at Zone 24×7. My research interests lie in the general area of Digital Signal Processing, particularly in Graph signal processing and Multi-dimensional signal processing. My current research and publications are related to graph filter and multi-dimensional digital filter design using convex optimization techniques. I am doing my research under the supervision of Dr. Chamira U. S. Edussooriya.
